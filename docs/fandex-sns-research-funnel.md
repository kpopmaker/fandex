# FANDEX SNS-Led Research Funnel

Status: positioning draft. No SNS API, automation, payment, login, or backend is
implemented.

## Why SNS Plus Research Site

FANDEX is unlikely to be discovered quickly as a standalone website. The sharper
commercial path is to publish timely K-pop and entertainment issue content on
SNS, then send interested readers to FANDEX for evidence, previews, sample
reports, and subscriber research.

Core positioning:

FANDEX is a research platform that verifies the latest K-pop and entertainment
industry issues with data and interprets them from a marketing perspective.

## Channel Roles

Instagram:

1. Card news for broad discovery.
2. Reels for short issue explanations.
3. Visual summaries of artist, brand, and comeback signals.

X:

1. Real-time issue interpretation.
2. Fast threads when K-pop or entertainment stories break.
3. Public notes that point to deeper FANDEX context.

LinkedIn:

1. Entertainment marketing analysis.
2. Brand collaboration and campaign positioning content.
3. Professional credibility for agencies, marketers, and industry watchers.

## Website Role

The website should act as the research layer behind SNS posts:

1. Verify the signal behind a social post.
2. Let visitors search or scan public previews.
3. Show a sample report.
4. Explain the free versus subscriber research boundary.
5. Convert high-intent readers into the waitlist.

## Free Vs Subscriber Boundary

Free Preview:

1. Artist quick search.
2. Public ranking snapshot.
3. Issue tone preview.
4. Sample report.
5. SNS signal archive preview.

Subscriber Research:

1. AI interpretation.
2. Full artist research brief.
3. Brand-fit analysis.
4. Issue risk analysis.
5. Artist comparison report.
6. Weekly FANDEX report.
7. Watchlist and signal commentary.

## Initial Content Ideas

1. Why one comeback teaser changed fan attention this week.
2. Which K-pop artist has the strongest brand-fit signal right now.
3. A quick read on chart momentum versus fandom momentum.
4. When controversy risk becomes a marketing monitoring issue.
5. How a brand ambassador announcement affects artist visibility.
6. IVE versus RIIZE: different signal profiles for different campaigns.
7. What entertainment marketers should watch before a comeback.
8. Why repeated headlines can increase volatility even without new facts.
9. How global fan reaction changes launch timing assumptions.
10. Weekly FANDEX signal watchlist preview.

## Funnel Draft

```text
SNS content
  -> profile link
  -> FANDEX free preview
  -> sample report
  -> waitlist
  -> subscriber research validation
```
