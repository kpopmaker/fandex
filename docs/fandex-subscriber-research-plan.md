# FANDEX Subscriber Research Plan Preview

Status: product structure preview. No payment, login, entitlement logic,
Supabase subscription table, AI backend, or report generation backend is
implemented.

## Purpose

FANDEX Subscriber Research explains what users can unlock after the free artist
search preview. The goal is to show that FANDEX can become a subscription
research product, not only a free signal search site.

Core idea:

1. Free search shows the surface signal.
2. Subscriber research explains why the signal matters.
3. Early Access validates which research outputs users will pay for later.

## Free Preview Vs Subscriber Research

Free Preview:

1. Artist quick search.
2. Public ranking snapshot.
3. Issue tone preview.
4. Sample report.
5. Limited signal preview.

Subscriber Research:

1. AI Interpretation.
2. Full Artist Research Brief.
3. Brand-fit Analysis.
4. Issue Risk Analysis.
5. Artist Comparison Report.
6. Weekly FANDEX Report.
7. Watchlist & Signal Commentary.

## Subscriber Feature Notes

AI Interpretation:
Extends preview signals into AI-assisted explanation and summary.

Full Artist Research Brief:
Combines fandom, activity, issue, chart, and brand signals into one document.

Brand-fit Analysis:
Frames brand collaboration, ambassador, and campaign-fit opportunities.

Issue Risk Analysis:
Interprets controversy, rumor, contract, hiatus, and concentration risk signals.

Artist Comparison Report:
Compares artists across fandom, brand, issue, and momentum signals.

Weekly FANDEX Report:
Packages weekly K-pop signal movement into a recurring research brief.

Watchlist & Signal Commentary:
Tracks priority artists and explains signal changes over time.
