# FANDEX Subscriber Research Plan Preview

Status: product structure preview. No payment, login, entitlement logic,
Supabase subscription table, AI backend, or report generation backend is
implemented.

The `/research` page now defaults to Korean so domestic users can understand the
Free, Plus, and Pro category unlock model without reading English-first product
copy. English remains available through the lightweight KO/EN toggle.

## Purpose

FANDEX Subscriber Research explains which paid categories users can unlock
after the limited free artist search preview. The `/research` route now acts as
the paid category unlock explanation page. The goal is to show that FANDEX can
become a subscription research product, not only a free signal search site.

Core idea:

1. Free search shows only the surface signal.
2. Subscriber research unlocks category-level context.
3. Early Access validates which research outputs users will pay for later.

## Subscriber Feature Notes

AI Interpretation:
Extends preview signals into AI-assisted explanation and summary.

Music / Album Signal:
Frames album, comeback, chart, and release momentum context.

News / Issue Signal:
Adds issue concentration and headline pattern context beyond the free tone.

SNS / Fandom Signal:
Tracks fandom attention, community movement, and social pickup.

Brand-fit Analysis:
Frames brand collaboration, ambassador, and campaign-fit opportunities.

Issue Risk Analysis:
Interprets controversy, rumor, contract, hiatus, and concentration risk signals.

Artist Comparison Report:
Compares artists across fandom, brand, issue, and momentum signals.

Weekly FANDEX Report:
Packages weekly K-pop signal movement into a recurring research brief.

Watchlist & Signal Commentary:
Tracks priority artists and explains signal changes over time.
